My Web Development Story

After leaving my previous job, I travelled through South East Asia for 9 months before finally coming to the Netherlands to settle. For a long time, while I was still under employment, I realised that I was in search for a new career venture in my life. A career with exciting prospects, and a ever changing world that would continuously fuel my thirst for growth. This search led me to coding! I started my journey watching Youtube videos on basic HTML skills and CSS, and reading my now favourite dev blog Team Treehouse. Next I moved on to study Free Code Camp, which helped reinforce my base knowledge. From those sources I built some pretty solid foundations. I then moved onto a Udemy course taught by Colt Steele, named Full Stack Development Bootcamp, this helped solidify the knowledge I gained early on and gave me a much better and thorough explanation of those topics. Not only that, it also brought me further into my journey by teaching me basic Javascript and jQuery.

I am currently completing the back end portion of the course, which involves nodeJS and Express. On top of this, I have just completed the first certification with Free Code Camp, Responsive Web Design Certification, and am soon to move onto study ES6 with FCC and Team Treehouse combined. Please check out my Free Code Camp / Team Treehouse profiles further down the page to check my progress :). Also during any spare time I have, I create my own projects using Wordpress, my first live project can be checked below in the "Portfolio" section, and try to implement new things I learn into my own Portfolio site.
